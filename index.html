Orientation

BOBCAT is a simple site that catalogs other sites that look good when rendered as text with the Lynx Web Browser.

Below is a list of web site which are usable by Lynx users. Simply scroll down until you see something interesting and either press <enter> to visit the site or press 'a', then 'l' to save the link to your bookmarks.

We also have a FAQ.

The Catalog

CNN Headlines
This is the "lite" version of the CNN web page. I bet it was created for older mobile devices, but it renders beautifully with Lynx and doesn't bombard you with ads.
Duck Duck Go Search
The good people at DuckDuckGo maintain a low complexity site that's easy to use and has zero cruft.
